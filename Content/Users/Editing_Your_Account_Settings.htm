Editing Your Account Settings

Which Permission is Required for This Activity?

No special permission is required to edit your profile and notification settings. To edit server settings, you need permissions (described below for each field).

 How to Edit your Account Settings
    • Top Navigation Click the drop-down next to your name and select Account Settings.
    • Users Page Click . The Users page opens. Hover over your avatar or name and from the user card, select Edit Profile.

    The Account Settings dialog opens.

  2. On the Profile tab, you can change or do any of the following:

    Profile Options

    Field

    Description

    [Avatar]

    Click on the image/square next to the “First Name” field if you want to choose an image or reset your avatar. In the dialog that opens, you can click Browse to find and select a new image (2 MB limit). You also have the option of selecting Reset, which will use your initials for the avatar. When finished, click OK.

    First Name

    Enter your first name. This field is required.

    Last Name

    Enter your last name. This field is required.

    Email Address

    This field displays your email address used for logging in. This field is required.

    Change Email

    Click this to change your email address. In the dialog that opens, complete the New Email Address field, then re-enter it in the Confirm Email Address field. When finished, click Save.

    Change Password

    Click this to change your password. In the dialog that opens, complete the Current Password field. Then complete the New Password and Confirm New Password fields.

    When finished, click Save.

    If you belong to more than one MadCap Central license, your password will be changed for all licenses.

    Initials

    You can enter your initials, which will be used if you have not selected an image for your avatar.

    Title

    You can enter your job title.

    Phone

    You can provide a phone number.

    Location

    You can enter a location, which might be useful if your company has several.

    Cell Phone

    You can provide your cell phone number.

    Department

    You can enter the department or organization where you work in your company.

    Secondary Email

    You can provide a secondary email. This might prove useful if co-workers need an additional way to contact you when you are out of the office.

  3. On the Notifications tab, you can choose any of the following in order to receive notifications for certain events:

    Receive notifications when Send Me Notifications By

    Field

    Description

    Notification Center

    Notifications can be seen in two areas in the interface: the Recent Notifications popup (accessed by clicking the bell icon at the top of the interface) and the Notification Center (accessed by selecting View All Notifications). See About Notifications.

    Select this option if you want to see notification alerts in the interface (i.e., the number of notifications will be indicated on the bell icon at the top of the interface).

    If you deselect this option, the numbers will not be displayed on the icon, but you can still open the popup to see notifications.

    Email

    Select this if you want to receive notifications via email. Notifications will be sent to both your primary and secondary email addresses (if the secondary email is provided).

  4. On the Server tab, depending on your permission settings, you can change or do any of the following:

    Server Options

    Field

    Description

    Key Label

    Only users with "Server Management" permission can change the key label.

    Changing the license key changes it for all users.

    Vanity URL

    See Setting Builds to “Live” or “Keep”.

    MadCap Central Expiration Date | Renew

    This section is visible only to users who have a yearly subscription that does not automatically renew. You can click Renew at any time to extend your subscription.

    Users | Add Users

    Select this if you wish to purchase user seats for your license. When you click this option, the Add to License dialog opens. See Purchasing User Seats and Storage Space.

    Storage | Add Storage

    Select this if you wish to purchase user seats for your license. When you click this option, the Add to License dialog opens. See Purchasing User Seats and Storage Space.
  5. Click Save.
