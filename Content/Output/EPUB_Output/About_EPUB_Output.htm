Reading Systems

reading systems note

Performance issues with EPUB may be caused by not having page or chapter breaks in your files, thus causing the output to consist of a single long document instead of several shorter ones. Other issues may arise due to rendering problems with different e-readers. See Troubleshooting EPUB Issues.
