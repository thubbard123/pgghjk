Collapsing Top Margins

In PDF and XPS targets you can collapse the top margin for all elements occurring at the top of a new page or column.

 how to collapse top margins
  1. Open the target.
  2. In the Target Editor, select the Advanced tab.
  3. In the Margins section, select Collapse top margin at top of page/column.
