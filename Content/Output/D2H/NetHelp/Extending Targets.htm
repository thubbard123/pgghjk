Extending Targets

With Doc-To-Help, you can extend your NetHelp targets further in two ways.

  • You can gather user feedback and  build a user community by incorporating DISQUS into NetHelp systems posted on the web. See Adding DISQUS Commenting to NetHelp Targets.
  • You can embed NetHelp targets (the full target or single topics) into web pages with or without iframes. See Embedding NetHelp Targets into Web Pages.
