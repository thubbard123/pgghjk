Renaming Destinations

Depending on the template you select when creating your project, may provide you with a default publishing destination. You can rename this destination or any additional destinations that you create for your project.

how to rename a destination
  2. Double-click the Destinations folder. The publishing destinations in your project are displayed.
  3. Click on the destination that you want to rename.
  4. Press F2 on your keyboard. The destination name is highlighted.
  5. Type a new name for the destination and press Enter.
