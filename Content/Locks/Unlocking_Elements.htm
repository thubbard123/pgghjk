Unlocking Elements

After locking elements of a paragraph, you can unlock them when you are ready to edit the text again. See Locking Elements.

how to unlock elements

  3. (Optional) If you want to remove the "marks" on paragraphs that prepare them for locking, place your cursor on the paragraph or highlight multiple paragraphs. Then select the Review ribbon. Select Lock>Lock to remove the check mark.
