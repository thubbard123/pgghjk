About Drawing Tools

After you create an image, you may want to make freehand additions or adjustments to it. You can use drawing tools to make these types of modifications to your images.

  • Color Fill Lets you fill selected areas of an image with color. See Using Color Fill.
  • Pencil Gives you the flexibility to create unstructured lines and drawings on your images. See Using the Pencil.
  • Eraser Allows you to manually erase unwanted content. See Erasing Image Areas.
