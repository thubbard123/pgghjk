About Cropping

You can crop images and image objects. When cropping images, you also have the option to save the image's history.

Ways to Crop
  • Images You can crop full images. See Cropping Images.
  • Image History You can choose whether or not to retain the image's cropping history. See Working with Image History.
  • Image Objects You can crop off sections of an image object. See Cropping Image Objects.
