About Effects

An effect is a special element or characteristic that you apply to an object to enhance it.

Ways to Use Effects
  • Adding Effects You can add an effect to an object. Although the effect is associated to the object, it can be adjusted to cover parts or all of the frame, as well. See Adding Effects.
  • Adding Shadow Effects See Adding Shadow Effects.
  • Editing Shadow Effects You can edit the position, color, and transparency of a shadow. See Editing Shadow Effects.
  • Editing Effects You can edit the start time of the effect, the duration of the effect, and the way effect is applied to the object. See Editing Effects.
  • Removing Effects You can remove effects from objects when they are no longer necessary. See Removing Effects.
