Creating Teams

How to Create a Team
  1. Click . The Teams page opens.
  2. In the toolbar click . The Create New Team dialog opens.
  3. Complete the Name field.
  4. (Optional) To the left of the field, click Change to find and select an image for the team avatar.
  5. (Optional) Complete the Description field.

  6. Using the grids below, choose the Flare project(s) and/or user(s) you want to associate with the team.

  7. Click Save and Finish.
