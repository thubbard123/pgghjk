About Notifications

Notifications in the Interface

Notifications can be seen in two areas in the interface:

  • Recent Notifications Popup When you click the bell icon at the top of the interface, the Recent Notifications popup shows the newest notifications.

  • Notification Center This is accessed by selecting View All Notifications in the Recent Notifications popup. This lets you see all of your notifications, not just the most recent ones.

When editing your account settings, select Notification Center if you want the bell icon at the top of the interface to indicate the number of recent notifications .

If you deselect this option, the numbers will not be displayed on the icon, but you can still open the popup to see notifications.

 Email Notifications

When editing your account settings, you can also instruct to send notifications to your email address.

Choosing Notifications to Receive

You can choose which notifications you want to receive, and how, by clicking your name at the top of the interface and editing your account settings. See Editing Your Account Settings.
