Get Started

When you break down the authoring process in Flare, you will discover that it can be quite simple. Following are the basic steps that you need to follow for creating and developing a project in .

Basic Steps
  1. Start Projects Create a project from scratch, or start a project by importing existing content from a variety of sources. See Starting Projects.
  2. Add Elements Add content and elements to your project, such as topics, text, a table of contents, cross-references, navigation, page layouts, and all of the other things necessary to help your end users. See Adding Elements and Objects.
  3. Make It Look Good Through the use of elements such as stylesheets, skins, and master pages, you can develop a look and feel for your output. See Making It Look Good.
  4. Develop Targets Decide the type(s) of output formats that you want to generate and design targets accordingly to meet your needs. See Developing Targets.
  5. Build and Distribute Output Generate output from a target, then make the files accessible to your end users. See Building and Publishing Output.
