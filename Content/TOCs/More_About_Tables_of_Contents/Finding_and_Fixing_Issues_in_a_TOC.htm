Finding and Fixing Issues in a Table of Contents

how to find and fix issues in a toc
  1. Open the TOC or outline. If the TOC contains unlinked items or broken links, it will be indicated in the local toolbar.
  2. Click to find and highlight the next issue in the TOC. To move to the previous issue, you can click .
  3. After you find an issue, you can take action, such as removing the item or clicking to open the properties where you can link the item to a file.

What if you do not want it to find the unlinked books, but rather the broken items only? In that case, simply click the Show Unlinked Books button . This hides the unlinked book icons. Therefore, when you click the Find Previous Warning or Find Next Warning button, it will skip over the unlinked books and move to the previous or next broken item.
