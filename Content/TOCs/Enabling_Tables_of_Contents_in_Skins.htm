Enabling TOCs in Skins

After you create a TOC manually or automatically, you need to enable TOCs in the skin that you intend to use for your target.

how to enable a toc in a skin
  1. Open the skin.
  2. On the General tab, click the TOC check box so that it contains a check mark.
 What's Next?

After you have finished enabling TOCs in a skin, you need to make sure the skin is associated with a target and the TOC is associated with a target. See Associating Skins with Targets and Associating a Master Table of Contents with a Target.
