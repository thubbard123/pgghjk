Getting Started

As a user, getting started with Pulse involves registering for an account, logging in, and creating your profile.

  • Register When you integrate a Pulse system with a Flare community, there are two methods for joining the community. Both methods require you to register and activate an account. See Registering for a Pulse Account.
  • Log In Once you have registered for a Pulse account, you can log in to the Pulse dashboard or you can log in to the published Flare output to use the community features. See Logging In to Pulse.
  • Edit Profile When you register for an account, a profile is created for you in the Pulse system. Registered Pulse users can edit their account profile by changing their personal information, uploading a profile images, and specifying notification settings. See Creating and Editing Your Profile.
