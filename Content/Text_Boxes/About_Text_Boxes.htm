When you insert a text box, you can make selections to affect the look of the box (e.g., border background, size). In addition, text boxes are based on div styles that you can select when inserting them. Therefore, you can always change the settings on a div style to automatically modify the look of all text boxes using that same div style. See Editing Text Boxes and About Styles and Stylesheets.
