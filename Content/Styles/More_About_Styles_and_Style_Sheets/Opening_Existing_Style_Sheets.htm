More About Regular Stylesheets

In addition to the information already covered, there are several other things you may do with regular stylesheets.

Opening Stylesheets

When you create a new stylesheet for your project, the stylesheet opens automatically in the Stylesheet Editor or Table Style Editor. If you want to make changes to a stylesheet that is closed, use the following steps to open it. You can open stylesheets that you create or that are part of new standalone documents that you create. However, you cannot open the stylesheets that are associated with contribution templates or topics sent to you by Flare authors.

 how to open a stylesheet that is part of a new document

The following steps pertain to new documents that you create without using a contribution template from a Flare author.

  1. Open the document that you created.
  2. In the tool strip menu, select Tools>Edit Stylesheet. The stylesheet opens in the Stylesheet Editor.
