Opening Snippets

There are a couple of ways to open a snippet file. One way is to open them from the Review Packages window pane (supported in either of the Review workflows). Another way is to open them from within a document (supported in all workflows).

how to open a snippet from the review packages window pane

You can double-click a snippet file in the grid and it opens in the XML Editor, just as a topic does.

 how to open a snippet from within a doucment
  1. Open a document where the snippet is inserted.
  2. Right-click on the snippet and select Open Link.The snippet opens in its own page of the XML Editor.
