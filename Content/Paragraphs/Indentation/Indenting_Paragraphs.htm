how to indent a paragraph using local formatting

The easiest way to indent a paragraph using local formatting is to simply click at the very beginning of a paragraph and press your Tab key. This indents the first line of the paragraph by 0.5 inch. See Creating Tabs.

However, if you want to use a different value than 0.5 inch, or if you want to indent the entire paragraph, use the following steps.

    • Ribbon At the bottom of the Paragraph section click .

    • Keyboard Shortcut Press CTRL+ALT+B.
    • Right-Click

    The Paragraph Properties dialog opens.

  3. Use the tab to specify the indentation settings.
