About Widows and Orphans

This feature is for print-based output.

  • Widow Control Determines the minimum number of lines in a paragraph to be left at the top of a page or column.
  • Orphan Control Determines the minimum number of lines in a paragraph to be left at the bottom of a page or column.

Tasks Associated with Widows and Orphans

tasks associated with widows and orphans

Following are the tasks that you may perform when it comes to widow and orphan control.

  • Enable Widow and Orphan Control   See Enabling and Disabling Widow/Orphan Control.
  • Set Widow and Orphan Control Settings By default, the setting for both widow and orphan control is 2. In other words, will always ensure that at least two lines are shown at the top or bottom of a page. If there is only one line, adjusts the text on the page accordingly. If you want to change the default setting, you can do so. See Setting Widow and Orphan Control.
