Selecting an Instant Messaging Status

how to select an instant messaging status

    • Ribbon In the Instant Messaging section select Instant Messages.

    • Keyboard Shortcut Press CTRL+SHIFT+I.

  2. Click the down arrow in the Available field and select the appropriate status.

    • Available
    • Away
    • Be Right Back
    • Busy
    • Invisible (If you select this option, your name will not be visible in the instant messaging interface for other Flare users on your network.)
    • On the Phone
    • Out to Lunch
