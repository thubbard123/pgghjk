Video Services Window Pane

The Video Services window pane opens by default on the left side of Mimic. This window pane allows you to upload videos to YouTube or Vimeo.

How to Open the Window Pane

  • In the Services section, select Video Services.

    The Video Service window pane opens.

 Tasks you can Perform in this Window Pane
  • Log In and Out to a Video Service You can log in and out of your YouTube or Vimeo accounts using Mimic. See Logging On to a Video Service.
  • Upload a Video You can upload a video to YouTube or Vimeo. During the upload process, you will be able to add basic information about the movie. This information will be part of the upload. See Uploading Movies to YouTube or Vimeo.
  • Edit Basic Video Information After uploading a video, you can go back and edit the basic information. See Editing Uploaded Movie Information.
  • View a Video You can view a video in your browser once you have uploaded it. See Viewing an Uploaded Movie.
  • Remove a Video You can use the Video Services window pane to remove a video from YouTube or Vimeo. See Removing an Uploaded Movie.
