Related Topics Window Pane

Use the Related Topics window pane to view and edit your related topics. You can view related topics three ways: by the hierarchy (TOC) structure, by keyword (index) entry, or by group.

Clicking on a topic, group, or keyword in the upper half of the Related Topics window pane will display related topics in the lower half. See Managing Related Topics. See the online Help or the Documents, Templates, and Content Guide.

You can right-click on a topic in the Hierarchy to do the following:

  • Change the sort to "by Document" or "Alphabetical."

  • Disable subtopic relations.
