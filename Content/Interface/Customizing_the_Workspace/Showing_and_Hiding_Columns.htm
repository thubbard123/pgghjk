Showing and Hiding Columns

How to Show and Hide Columns in a Grid
  1. In Central, open a page with a grid—Projects, Tasks (Calendar, Archive, or Backlog), Teams, Users.

  2. In the toolbar, click Column Options. The Column Options dialog opens.

    If you do not see this option in the local toolbar, you cannot show or hide columns for the current page.

  3. (Optional) To show columns, in the Available Columns list, select the column(s) you want to add. Click to move them to the Selected Columns grid.
  4. (Optional) To hide columns, in the Selected Columns list, select the column(s) you want to remove. Click to move them to the Available Columns grid.
  5. (Optional) To rearrange columns, in the Selected Columns list, select a column, then click or to move it up or down in the list of columns.

  6. Click Save and Finish. The columns on the page will be updated.
