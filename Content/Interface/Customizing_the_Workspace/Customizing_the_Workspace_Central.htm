Customizing the Workspace

One of the benefits of 's user interface is that it is flexible and easy to customize to meet your needs. Following are some of the ways you can customize the workspace.

Adding Widgets to the Dashboard

You can add widgets to the Central dashboard to customize the information you can see at-a-glance. Once you add a widget to the dashboard, you can move it, resize it, or filter it to further customize how the widget is displayed.
