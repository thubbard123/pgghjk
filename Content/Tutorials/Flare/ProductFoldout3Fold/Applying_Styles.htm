Applying Styles

Now we are going to apply styles to the remaining content in our topics that need it. In addition to previously existing styles, we will also apply the two new style clases you created.

Apply Styles to the Front Topic

Apply Styles to the Front Topic

Most of the work is already done in the front topic, but we need to apply styles to the first two paragraphs, which serve as the cover for the foldout.

  1. Select the Home ribbon, and in the Styles section click Style Window. The window pane opens on the right side of the interface.

  2. Open the Front2.htm topic.

  3. Click in the first paragraph, where the "CompanyName" variable is inserted. Do not select the variable itself, but rather click either right before or after it. Notice that the Styles window populates with all of the styles that you are allowed to select.

  4. In the Styles window pane, scroll down and select ManualTitle1New, the first style class you created.

    In the topic, the first paragraph is formatted, with extra space above and to the left of it.

  5. Click in the second paragraph, just before or after the variable.

  6. In the Styles window pane, select p.ManualTitle2New, the second style class you created.

    In the topic, the second paragraph is formatted accordingly.

Apply Styles to the Back Topic

Apply Styles to the Back Topic

In the other topic, we are going to apply styles that previously existed in the stylesheet.

  1. Open the Back2.htm topic.

  2. Use the same steps above to apply the following styles to the first four paragraphs in the Back2 topic:

    • This is the Back Panel Apply the p.BackPanel style class.
    • CompanyName Variable Apply the p.CompanyInfo1 style class.
    • StreetAddress Variable Apply the p.CompanyInfo2 style class.
    • CityStateZip Variable Apply the p.CompanyInfo2 style class.
    • www.madcapsoftware.com Apply the p.CompanyInfo2 style class.
