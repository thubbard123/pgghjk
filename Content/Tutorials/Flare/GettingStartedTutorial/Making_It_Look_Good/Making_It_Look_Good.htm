Making It Look Good

There are lots of ways in Flare to make your output look good. In this tutorial we're going to focus on the most common way to control the look of your documentation—styles. However, keep in mind that this tutorial only scratches the surface of how to use styles.

The following style information is covered in this tutorial:

  1. Creating Style Classes You can use styles in your projects to format elements. See Creating Style Classes.
  2. Applying Styles After you create a style class, you can apply it to content in your topics. See Applying Styles.
