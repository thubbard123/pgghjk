Build and View Output
  1. In the Project Organizer, double-click the HTML5 target to open it.

  2. In the local toolbar of the Target Editor, click .

    The Builds window pane opens at the bottom of the Flare interface, and the target begins to generate.

    When the build finishes successfully, the Build Progress and Compile Progress cells turn dark green.
