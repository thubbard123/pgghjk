Automatically Adding Cursors to Recorded Movies

When you record a movie, Mimic can automatically capture your mouse movements. In your movie frames, this is reflected by the addition of a cursor with a trajectory (path). When the movie is generated and played, the cursor moves just as you moved it when creating the movie.

how to Automatically Add cursors to recorded movies

  1. Select File>Recording Options.

    The Recording Options dialog opens.

    You can also open this dialog from the Options button at the bottom of the recording area. 

  2. In the Options section, click Record cursor movements.
  4. Record the movie. See Recording Movies.
 Note

You can also add cursors to frames manually. See Adding Cursors.

 What's Next?

If you automatically add cursors to your movies, you may need to edit their trajectories. See Editing Cursor Trajectories.
