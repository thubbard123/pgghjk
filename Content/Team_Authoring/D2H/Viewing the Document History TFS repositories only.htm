Viewing the Document History

In TFS repositories, you can view the history of a document.

How to View the Document History
  1. In the Team Authoring window, select the file.
  2. Click the History button. The History dialog opens.
  3. Review the information and click the Cancel or Apply button.
 Notes

You can also enter a comment on a file using this dialog.

For other tasks, see Working in a Team Project.
