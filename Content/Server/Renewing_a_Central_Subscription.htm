Renewing a Central Subscription

Users who have the appropriate permissions can manage certain server settings.

Note

How to Renew a Central Subscription

    • Home Page Click . The Home page opens. In the Storage and Usage widget, click Renew.

      OR

    • Settings Dialog In the main navigation framework, click your user name, then click Settings. The Settings dialog opens. Click the Server tab. Under the MadCap Central Expiration Date graph, click Renew.

    The Add to License dialog opens.

  2. In the dialog, use the drop-downs to select the products you want to add to your license, and to select the type of renewal you want for your license.

    As you add or remove user licenses and/or storage space, Central calculates the total cost and displays it next to the Total Due Today field.

    The total shown does not include tax.

  4. Click Continue. The Confirm Purchase dialog opens. A message shows you the number of user licenses and/or the amount of storage space you are purchasing, as well as the total amount of the purchase (exclusive of tax).
  5. Click Complete Purchase. Your card will be charged.
