Inserting Blank Frames

You can insert a blank frame after the selected frame. You can then add a background image or color, as well as objects to that frame

how to Insert a Blank frame
  1. In the Frames window pane, select the frame that will precede the blank frame.

    • Ribbon In the Insert section, select Blank.

    • Local Toolbar Click .

    The new blank frame is added below the current frame.

 notes

You can also insert frames by recording or capturing new frames. See Recording New Frames and Capturing New Frames.

You can also insert Microsoft PowerPoint slides into a movie. See Inserting PowerPoint Slides into a Movie.
